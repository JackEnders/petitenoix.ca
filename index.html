Flavour comes first

Flavour comes first at Petite Noix Pâtisserie.

From freshly squeezed lemons, to single origin chocolate. Each bite is crafted with layers of handmade tastebud tingling elements. Our kitchen is more of a laboratory experimenting with modern culinary techniques, inspired by traditional French methodolgy, and infusing flavours from around the globe.

Petite Noix is currently baked out of Red Brick Cafe, on 8 Douglas St, Guelph, Ontario.

Looking for something custom?

We will collaborate to make memorable sweets for any occasion or situation. Free delivery is available to Guelph, Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo and nearby townships. Curious about arranging a tasting or commissioning a custom product?
